What is Blood Sugar?

Blood sugar (glucose) is the main sugar found in your blood and primary energy source for your body. Maintaining healthy sugar levels is essential for overall metabolism, energy, and organ function.

Why Blood Sugar Control is Important

High blood sugar can lead to diabetes, nerve damage, kidney disease, and cardiovascular problems. Controlling sugar levels through diet, lifestyle, and natural remedies is vital.

Common Blood Sugar Disorders

  • Type 1 Diabetes
  • Type 2 Diabetes
  • Prediabetes
  • Insulin resistance
