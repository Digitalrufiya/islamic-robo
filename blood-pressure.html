What is Blood Pressure?

Blood pressure is the force of blood pushing against the walls of your arteries. Maintaining a healthy blood pressure is crucial for heart, kidney, and brain health.

Why Blood Pressure Control is Important

High blood pressure increases the risk of heart attack, stroke, and kidney disease. Lifestyle changes and natural remedies can help maintain healthy blood pressure.

Common Blood Pressure Disorders

  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Hypotension (low blood pressure)
  • Secondary hypertension (due to kidney or hormonal issues)
