What is the Immune System?

The immune system is the body’s defense mechanism against harmful bacteria, viruses, fungi, and other pathogens. It is a complex network of cells, tissues, and organs that work together to maintain health and protect the body from infections.

Why it’s Important

A strong immune system helps prevent illnesses, recover faster from infections, and maintain overall wellness. A weakened immune system can lead to frequent sickness or chronic health issues.

Common Immune System Disorders

  • Autoimmune diseases (e.g., rheumatoid arthritis, lupus)
  • Immunodeficiency disorders (e.g., primary immunodeficiency, HIV)
  • Allergies and hypersensitivities
